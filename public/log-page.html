Log Sighting

How to use

You can use the search function in the top right to search for a bird by name. If you're unsure, I recommend using the 'Merlin' app to help narrow down your search.

The checkmark will count as you saying you have sighted the featured bird. The browser will save your data so you don't have to worry about losing your sightings!

The comment box is for anything you wish to put there really; for instance, where the sighting took place, what time, or even what you were doing at the time! It can be fun to look back and remember that exact moment.

The final element is the status area. It features the conservation status of the selected bird, be it *****PUT THE STATUSES HERE***** For reference there is a graphic in the top corner to briefly explain the different statuses.

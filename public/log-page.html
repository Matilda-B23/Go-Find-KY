Log Sighting

How to use

Use the search function at the top of the page to search for a bird by name. If you're unsure, I recommend using the 'Merlin' app to help narrow down your search.

The checkmark tracks birds you have sighted. Your browser will save your data so you don't have to worry about losing your sightings.

The comment box is an optional feature. You can add notes about where, when, or what you were doing during your sighting. If you don't wish to leave a comment, simply press save.

The status area shows the conservation status of the selected bird: from low concern (birds with healthy populations) to declining populations, to species threatened with extinction.
